With WebTV Remix, we allow ads for smaller projects to be run, which will be randomly picked from all current ads to be shown to all our users on our AD page. However, there are a few guidelines you need to follow for your ad to be accepted.

#3: Your ad must have an associated web site

If your ad is on WebTV Remix, it'll need a URL that users can go to for more information. You don't have to create an entire web site for your ad to be accepted, but you do need to have at least one page giving more information about your project that loads fast on WebTV (Under 30 seconds on a box over dial-up, not including images). It also has to look nice on WebTV, you can use a standard text page, or a page with images, but formatting issues will cause your ad to be rejected. To make a page that works in WebTV, you can use FrontPage 2003 and the viewer, or manually write a simple page.
